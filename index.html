Answer a series of questions related to your living preferences and personality and Hoodie will tell you the best Chicago neighborhood fit for you. I worked on this application with a group and was in charge of the front end logic and UI as well as server side logic and database structure. Technologies we used included HTML, Materialize CSS, Javascript, jQuery, MySQL, Node and Express.
